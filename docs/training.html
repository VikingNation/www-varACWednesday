Voice Announcement Setup

Voice announcements are a great way to hear what is going on with VarAC. They are not so great when they are transmitted over the air and cause QRM for fellow operators. Check-out our Voice Announcement Setup training and you will be enjoying those VarAC announcements without transmitting them over the air.
