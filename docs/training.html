Default Speaker Setting in Windows

It is important to properly setup your default speaker settings in Windows. Voice announcements are a great way to hear what is going on with VarAC. They are not so great when they are transmitted over the air and cause QRM for fellow operators. Check-out our "Are you sending voice annoncements over the air?" training and you will be enjoying those VarAC announcements without transmitting them over the air.
