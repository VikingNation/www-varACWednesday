GPS coordinates in check-in messages

August 30, 2025

We curretly look up participants' callsigns on QRZ.com to obtain GPS coordinate for plotting check-ins on the map. QRZ might contain outdated location data. Some operator operate portable and their location might change from week to week. Starting September 3rd, 2025 we will begin extracting GPS locations from check-in messages. Jason Johnson's VarAC GPS Setup (Static location) training walks through how to configure your VarAC settings to start uing this new feature.
