What is VarAC Wednesday?

VarAC Wednesday is a weekly amateur radio digital net where users check-in by sending a message via a station providing EMAIL gateway service.

How do I check-in to VarAC Wednesday?

Please see our instructions guide for a full walk thru.

What is the relationship to Winlink Wednesday?

VarAC Wednesday has no connection or relationship with the original Winlink Wednesday or any other organization who conduct Winlink teting. VarAC Wednesday uses the same message format as Winlink Wednesday. We encourage operators to participate in both weekly digital nets.
